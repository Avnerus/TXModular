LFO


Module type:  Control Source


Introduction:

Low Frequency Oscillator for modulating parameters of other modules. 


Controls:

frequency - the rate of change of the waveform

waveform - selects different kinds of waveforms including some random ones. 

Note: Some of the random LFOs have "Random values -" in their name, and others have  "Random values and timing-". 

The difference it that those that just have random values will move to their new values at regular intervals (depending on their frequency). Whereas those that have random timing as well will move to their new values at irregular intervals - so their "frequency" really means "average frequency".

output range - preset output ranges including negative values


Control Modulation Input Busses:

frequency
